How to Use AlertSense

AlertSense is a simple emergency sound detector that helps keep you safe by alerting you to whistles, cries, sirens, or someone shouting for help.

  1. Open the app and tap Start Listening to activate the microphone.
  2. Keep your device nearby during disaster-prone situations.
  3. If an emergency sound is detected, the screen will flash red, the device may vibrate, and a warning message will appear.
  4. Tap Stop Listening to turn off the microphone and stop alerts.
