üìñ How to Use AlertSense

  1. Grant Microphone Access

    Allow AlertSense to access your microphone when prompted. This is required for sound detection.

  2. Set Emergency Contact

    Go to Settings and add an emergency contact number. This person will be called automatically when danger is detected.

  3. Start Listening

    Tap the "Start Listening" button to activate sound detection. Keep your device nearby.

  4. Stay Alert

    When an emergency sound is detected, your screen will flash red, your device will vibrate, and your emergency contact will be called.

  5. Stop When Safe

    Tap "Stop Listening" when you no longer need monitoring. This saves battery and prevents false alerts.
